I am a User Experience researcher. I received my Ph.D. from the University of Trento (Italy) where I collaborated with the KnowDive group and the interAction Lab. My area of research was engagement and participation. More concretely, I investigated what makes people contribute to online collaborative approaches and how adopting a reflexive orientation can support the investigation of engagement.

During my doctoral studies I was part of the ESSENCE-network a Marie Curie Initial Training Network. Moreover, I was awarded with a personal grant by the Sorbonne Univesité that allowed me to visit KAIST the sixth most innovative university in the world, according to Thomson Reuters. During my visit to KAIST I collaborated with Juho Kim (Ph.D. by MIT) head of KixLab.

Before, I was an eHealth Researcher in Barcelona Digital (Spain) in the eHealth department . I worked in the Synergy-COPD project where I researched, designed and implemented a user interface to facilitate the interaction between health practioners and physiology models.

I got my Master in Artificial Intelligence from the Heriot-Watt University Edinburgh (UK) and my Bachelors and Master in Computer Engineering from Complutense University of Madrid (Spain).
