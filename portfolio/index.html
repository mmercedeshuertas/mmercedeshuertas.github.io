Co-Design of an Online Repository for Instructional Material

The project investigated participation in the co-design of an online repository to store educational materials. In this case, participants made use of the available AI facilitate their practices. The project was located in Trentino a Northern region in Italy, where the educational system was going through a deep change. After a deep ethnographic research through interviews, focus groups, and interaction with a mobile app to facilitate materials´ organisation. All this qualitative data was analysed to understand participants’ experience, practices and expectations. Then, they were involved in the co-design of an online repository through workshops based on design-thinking methodology. Finally, the designs were implemented in Google Drive and tested by them.

Linguarena

The project investigated participation through contribution to co-design of a collaborative system. In this case, such contribution aims to supports the need for data in AI systems. The project involved participants to create an online linguistic resource through translation. In a exploratory study, a group of participants were involved in the evaluation of a preliminary version of the UI. Their insights and the analysis of their interaction were translated into actionable design directions to create a paper prototype. A second set of participants was involved in its evaluation and a focus group. With their insights, the UI was redesigned and tested by the participants.

Synergy-COPD

Synergy-COPD was a FP7 project whose aim was to develop different tools to facilitate clinical practices and biomedical research. One of the outcomes of the project was the research, design, and implementation of a UI that would facilitate the interaction between practitioners and physiological models. Through in-depth interviews with different stakeholders a UI was designed, developed, and tested.
