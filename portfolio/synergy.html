Synergy-COPD

Synergy-COPD was a FP7 project whose aim was to develop different tools to facilitate clinical practices and biomedical research. One of the outcomes of the project was the research, design, and implementation of a UI that would facilitate the interaction between practitioners and physiological models. Through in-depth interviews with different stakeholders a UI was designed, developed, and tested.
